DESCRIPTION

Made from plastic bottles, the Do Good Hoodie gives new life to material once destined for the landfill. This process starts by shredding plastic bottles into flakes which are then melted into pellets. Those pellets are then melted into yarn that yearns to do more. Now plastic gets a new life through this unique, ultra-soft hoodie.
